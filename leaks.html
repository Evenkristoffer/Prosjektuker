Grand Theft Auto VI Leaks

This page collects short notes about major GTA 6 leaks and early releases that were widely reported.

Overview

September 18-19, 2022: early GTA 6 development footage leaked online. Reports described around ninety clips showing unfinished gameplay, debug views, city movement, police encounters, and robbery scenes.

The 2022 leak also pushed discussion around a two-protagonist setup. Rockstar's official GTA VI page later confirmed Jason and Lucia, which matched what many people were already talking about after the footage leak.

December 4, 2023: Trailer 1 leaked online before Rockstar's planned release time. Rockstar then published the trailer early. AP reported that it went live about fifteen hours ahead of schedule because of the leak.

Rockstar's public copyright policy says pre-release leaked footage is not allowed to be reposted, so this page summarizes the leaks instead of embedding or linking to leaked clips.

Sources: Rockstar GTA VI / AP News / Rockstar policy
